# variant-04 - professional-recommendation

## Voiceover

Here is the working sequence. Keep the clear film folded and press the yellow tape firmly along the cabinet edge first. After the tape is secure, pull the film down to cover the tile and countertop. Fix the far edge and tear by hand. The film protects the work area from paint and dust, then peels away with the dirt when the job is finished.

## Scenes

1. A PAINTER'S PICK | 8s | One roll for faster masking.
2. INTEGRATED ROLL | 5s | Yellow tape plus clear PE film.
3. MY FIRST STEP | 4s | Press the yellow tape firmly.
4. THEN COVER | 4s | Pull the clear film down.
5. TEAR BY HAND | 4.2s | No scissors needed.
6. READY TO WORK | 3.2s | Paint and dust stay on the film.
7. PEEL AWAY | 4.4s | Remove the dirty covering.
8. PRO RESULT | 3s | A cleaner finish with less cleanup.
