# variant-03 - product-structure

## Voiceover

One roll does two jobs. The yellow masking tape and clear PE film are permanently connected, so the product stays together while you work. Press the yellow tape along the cabinet edge first. Then pull the film down, cover the surface, fix the far edge, and tear by hand. Paint and dust land on the film, and the dirty covering peels away when the job is done.

2. INTEGRATED DESIGN | 5s | The tape and film stay connected.
3. TAPE FIRST | 4s | Press the yellow edge firmly.
4. FILM SECOND | 4s | Pull the clear PE film down.

6. SURFACE BARRIER | 3.2s | Paint and dust collect on the film.
7. REMOVE TOGETHER | 4.4s | Tape, film, and mess come away together.
8. ONE SMARTER ROLL | 8s | Try the integrated masking system.
