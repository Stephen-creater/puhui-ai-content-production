# variant-05 - before-after

## Voiceover

Before masking, paint and sanding dust can reach the countertop and tile. After using pre-taped masking film, the mess leaves with the covering. Press the connected yellow tape first, then pull the clear PE film down. Fix the far edge and tear by hand. Work over the protected surface, then peel the film away to reveal the clean area underneath.
