# variant-02 - efficiency

## Voiceover

Mask the work area with fewer separate steps. Start by pressing the built-in yellow tape firmly along the cabinet edge. Once it is secure, pull the clear PE film down to cover the tile and countertop. Fix the far edge and tear by hand. The film catches paint and dust, then peels away with the mess, leaving far less cleanup after the job.

## Scenes

1. MASK FASTER | 3s | Fewer separate masking steps.
2. PRESS ONCE | 4s | Secure the built-in yellow tape first.
3. COVER MORE | 4s | Pull the clear film down smoothly.
4. NO SCISSORS | 4.2s | Fix the edge and tear by hand.
5. ONE INTEGRATED ROLL | 5s | Tape and film stay connected.
6. KEEP WORK MOVING | 3.2s | Paint and dust collect on the film.
7. CLEAN UP FASTER | 4.4s | Peel away the dirty film.
8. FASTER MASKING | 8s | Spend less time on cleanup.
