# variant-01 - pain-result

## Voiceover

Painting or sanding? Skip hours of cleanup. Pre-taped masking film combines yellow tape and clear PE film in one roll. First press the yellow tape firmly along the cabinet edge. Then pull the film down to cover the tile and countertop. Fix the far edge, pull tight, and tear by hand. Paint and dust stay on the film. Peel it away with the mess and leave far less cleanup behind.
